ABOUT DISTRICT

District was created by a Philippine Science High School Scholar with a goal in mind. It is a platform which aims to showcase a collection of written art that targets socio-economic issues as the human race fastens its gaze towards a brighter tomorrow. With the ever-increasing number of social, political, and economic issues which arise, many Filipinos are often the target of the enemy.

GWYNETH DAVID

PSHS SCHOLAR

ABOUT THE AUTHOR

Gwyneth David is currently in her 3rd year at PSHS-CLC. She believes that awareness and solidarity will aid the people in their social endeavors. She started District to help spread awareness and help fight the major conflicts which surround the country.
