OUR VISION AND MISSION

VISION

District's vision is to resolve present and prevalent social issues that have had huge impacts in the country. Its vision is to become the voice of the voiceless and bestow power among the youth through writing. We believe that even in the midst of such horrible chaos, we can still find peace within ourselves.

OUR VISION AND MISSION

MISSION

District believes in the power which lies inside of us. It believes that even though the world may seem difficult to understand, we can still find solace in the stars and in the many individuals who continually fight alongside us. District's prime mission is to bridge the gap that is present in society. Its primary aim is to serve and share information and insights with one another.
